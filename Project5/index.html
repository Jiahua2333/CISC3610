Voice Recognition

This website is about voice recognition. You can change something when you speak some keywords.
Click Start to ready speak. Click Stop to stop or Click cancel to cancel voice recognition.
After pressed start button,
you can say COLOR, followed by a color, to set the color of the circle. You can say BACKGROUND, followed by a color, to set the color of the background.
You can also say SIZE, followed by a number from 1 to 300, to set the diameter of the circle. If you say the word, HELP, you can hear the instruction to use.
In addition, you can say the word, ABOUT, to hear the description of this website.
